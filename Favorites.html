Favorites

Favorite Movie Series



My favorite movie series is the Star Wars Saga. My love for the Star Wars universe goes much past the movies, however. I watch the TV shows, no matter how childish they are sometimes, I've read plenty of Star Wars Books, and I've played almost every Star Wars videogame known to man. I'm a firm believer in the Jar Jar theory, and my favorite character is Obi-wan Kenobi.

Favorite TV Show



I'll have to give this one to Rick and Morty. Rick's briliant, sarcastic humor never fails to make me laugh. The best part about the show, though, is its unspoken backstory played through subtle hints in each episode. Be careful when watching this show, if you're not thinking critically, you might just miss something. In honorable mention, Game of Thrones takes a very close second. Wuba. Luba. DubDub.

Favorite Book



This one may actually be a tie between the Lord of the Rings series and the Star Wars: Darth Bane trilogy. I'm going to go with the Lord of the Rings solely because the classic fantasy world that millions of people have grown to love wouldn't be what it is today without the briliant mind of author J.R.R. Tolkien. Fun fact, the famous quote everyone knows from Gandalf in The Fellowship of the Ring as he wards off the Balrog Demon was actually written "You cannot pass" in the book, but "you shall not pass" was written in the movie script for cinematic effect.
